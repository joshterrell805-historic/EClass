Joel Wilcox Navigate between slides. After selecting a presentation to open, you can click the 'Previous', 'Next', or 'SYNC' buttons to change slides. You can also enter a number in the text box which says 'Slide Number' to go to a specific slide. Clicking 'Previous' will change to the previous slide (if there is one). Clicking 'Next' will change to the next slide (if there is one). Clicking 'SYNC' will currently only print a message to console until we are further along with networking. Entering a valid slide number into the 'Slide Number' text box will change to that slide. This can all be seen visually as the controls are used. (NOTE: The 'SYNC' button will only be visible if you log in with a student id like 'student')
Joel Wilcox View and change a Student's permissions. With the Roster window open, click on the black arrow next to a student's name. Then click on the 'Permissions' button and make any desired changes to the radio buttons and check-boxes. Then click 'Accept' to save the permissions or 'Cancel' to discard the changes. After closing the Permissions window, click on the 'Permissions' button again to reopen it and view the changes if any were made. The new permissions can be seen by the radio button and check-box states. If changes were made but discarded, you'll be able to see that the permissions are the same as they were initially.

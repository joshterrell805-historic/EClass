Sorts the list of students in the Roster as well as the sublists of present students and absent students by first name.

Preconditions:
  • !self.students.isEmpty()
  • !self.presentStudents.isEmpty()
  • !self.absentStudents.isEmpty()
Postconditions:
  • self.students.isSorted()
  • self.presentStudents.isSorted()

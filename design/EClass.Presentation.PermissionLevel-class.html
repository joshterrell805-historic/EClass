Class PermissionLevel

source code

A PermissionLevel is an Enum of the three presentation permission levels used for students.

Author: Joel Wilcox (jnwilcox@calpoly.edu)

Instance Variables [hide private]
  Lockdown
The student may not draw on his own layers on his own machine and he may not push a layer.
  Normal
The student may draw on his own layers, and may ask to add his layer to the presenter's layer stack by "push"ing that layer.
  Unrestricted
The student may draw on his own layers and push layers to the public stack freely.

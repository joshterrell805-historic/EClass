2.5. Presenter/Student Interactions

The Presenter/Student Interactions encompasses the various ways in EClass that the presenter and the students are able to interact with each other during the presentation. A student asking a question or sending a layer is a direct interaction with the presenter, while the forum is intended for the students.

  • Presenter Question Management
  • Student Sending and Presenter Receiving of Layers

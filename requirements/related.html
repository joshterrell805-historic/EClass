1.5. Related Systems

Some systems that have similar functionality to EClass include

  • WebEx
  • HeuX
  • Blackboard Virtual Classroom
  • Saba
  • Learnspace
  • Gimp
All of these solutions provide parts of the functionality, but none of them truly encompass the scope of EClass. While most of the solutions provide a good amount of the functionality that the student and teacher want, they don't provide tools that help the lecturer to present or the student to learn. EClass both provides a way for class to view the lesson and a variety of tools that help everyone in the classroom. These tools include:
  • Collapsable Lectures

  • Kick out a Student

  • Track Approval Rating
